The Problem

Attention is the internet's real currency —
but the people who make it own none of it.

Creators get nothing tradable

A post can reach millions and the author walks away with karma points. The value accrues to the platform, not the person.

Early spotters aren't rewarded

The people with the taste to catch something before it goes viral have no way to back that conviction and share in the outcome.

Attention isn't ownable

Virality is locked inside walled gardens. It can't be priced, traded, or carried anywhere — so none of it compounds for the community.

The Solution

KarmaFi makes attention a market.

Any Reddit post can be launched as its own BEP-20 token, priced live on an on-chain bonding curve. Back content early and share in its rise — and a fixed share of every trade is reserved on-chain for the original creator to claim.

Price discovery from block one

A linear bonding curve means a market exists the moment a post is launched — no order book, no seeding, instant liquidity.

Creators are paid, on-chain

A reserved cut of all trading fees is held for the verified original author — aligning creators and the crowd, not pitting them against each other.

Winners graduate

When a market matures, its liquidity migrates to a PancakeSwap V2 pool with permanently burned LP — a real, community-owned token.

KarmaFi
03
How It Works

From a post to a permanent token — in four steps.

1

Launch

A curator turns a Reddit post into a market. The curator pays gas — no cost to the platform per launch.

2

Trade

The token trades on a bonding curve with a 1% fee. Price rises with demand, fully on-chain.

3

Claim

The original Reddit author verifies ownership and claims their reserved share of accrued fees.

4

Graduate

Past a reserve threshold, liquidity auto-migrates to PancakeSwap V2 and the LP is burned forever.

Business Model

Revenue scales directly with attention traded.

More markets → more trades → more fees. Each graduated token becomes a standing, liquid asset that keeps generating volume, while the creator-reward loop pulls in the people who make content go viral in the first place. The flywheel is attention itself.
